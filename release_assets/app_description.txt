Siddha: A Simple Journey

Siddha isn't about complex rituals or overflowing content libraries. It’s a straightforward, gamified meditation tracker designed to help you build a consistent habit.

What you get:
- The Anicha Journey: A step-by-step path to visualize your progress.
- Simple tracking: Log your meditations without the clutter.
- Honest design: A clean, distraction-free interface that gets out of your way.

That's it. No subscriptions to unlock basic features, and no endless notifications. Just a simple tool to support your practice.
