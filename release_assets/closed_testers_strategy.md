# Closed Testers Acquisition Strategy: Siddha

To release your app on the Google Play Store (for individual developer accounts created after November 2023), Google requires **20 closed testers** to be opted-in to your closed test track continuously for at least **14 days**.

Here are the best ways to find the remaining testers, along with a ready-to-use email invite template for your waitlist.

---

## 📈 1. Target Your Landing Page Waitlist (Best Source)
Since you already have a waitlist set up (stored via Loops.so and waitlist database), these users are already interested in Siddha. Sending a clean, personal invitation is your highest-conversion channel.

### Email Invitation Template (Copy & Paste):
```text
Subject: You're invited: Siddha Closed Beta is now live! 🧘

Hi Practitioner,

Thanks for signing up for the Siddha early access waitlist. We are excited to let you know that the first closed beta build is ready for Android!

Siddha is a simple, private, and gamified meditation tracker built to help you cultivate consistency.

To help us launch on the Google Play Store, we need a small group of early testers to run the app for 14 days and share their honest thoughts.

How to join the closed test:
1. Reply to this email with your Google Play Account email address (the Gmail you use on your Android phone) so we can add you to the authorized testers list.
2. Once added, we will send you the Google Play join link to download the app directly from the Play Store.

Thank you so much for supporting this journey and helping us build a distraction-free space for mindfulness.

Peace,
The Siddha Team
```

---

## 🤝 2. Developer Exchange Communities (Fastest Exchange)
If you need to quickly fill the slots, other developers are in the exact same position and trade testing services (1-for-1 exchanges).

- **Reddit communities**:
  *   **`r/AndroidClosedTesting`**: Specifically created for developers to trade tester access. Post your Google Group/join links here, and offer to test their apps in return.
  *   **`r/playtesters`**: General game/app playtesting requests.
  *   **`r/androidapps`**: Share a self-promotion post (on weekends/designated days) inviting users to test.
- **Google Group Cross-Testing**:
  Create a public Google Group (e.g. `siddha-beta-testers@googlegroups.com`), add it to your Google Play Console closed testing track, and share the Google Group link on these forums. Anyone who joins the group instantly gains access to the Play Store download link.

---

## 🌸 3. Organic Mindfulness Communities (High Retention)
Engage in spaces where your target demographic hangs out. Focus on offering value and introducing Siddha as a free, privacy-first alternative.

- **Reddit subreddits**:
  *   **`r/meditation`**, **`r/mindfulness`**, and **`r/Zen`**: Share your journey of building a distraction-free, zero-subscription tool. Many members are frustrated with commercialized, expensive subscription models and will gladly support an independent offline tracker.
- **Mindfulness Discord Servers**: Join wellness/meditation groups and request playtesters in the `#off-topic` or `#projects` channels.
