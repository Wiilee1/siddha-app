Privacy Policy for Siddha Meditation

Last updated: July 2026

Siddha Meditation is built with privacy as a core value. Below you will find the complete details of how your data is handled.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Siddha Meditation app runs entirely client-side on your device. All of your personal details (such as your name), meditation session history, daily quests progress, mood reflections, and milestones are stored locally on your device via standard browser storage (localStorage).

We do not host any remote databases, do not track user behavior, and do not transmit or store any of your personal meditation logs or entries on remote servers.

2. Third-Party Sharing

Because your data is saved strictly locally on your device, we do not share, sell, distribute, or make your personal information or meditation history accessible to any third-party entities or advertising networks.

3. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete all of your progress, streak data, and history at any time by selecting the "Reset Progress" option in the app's Profile screen, or by clearing the application storage/cache within your mobile device's settings menu.

4. Children's Privacy

Siddha does not collect any data and is safe for all age groups, though it is marketed toward mature mindfulness practitioners.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy page from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by editing this document and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of the page.
